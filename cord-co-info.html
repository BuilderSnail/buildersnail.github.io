About

Cord CO. is a railway service managed by Shootz_. A form for signing up to the service can be found here. This is free and allows a bunch of options.

Services

Cord Co. gives you 3 services to choose from:

CORD CO. MAIL

Have mail taken to you or someone else!

CORD CO. BULK

Allows buying items and having them transported straight to your doorstep.

CORD CO. COMMUTER

A passenger train going around spawn
